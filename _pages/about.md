Wei Chen graduated from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2025. His research interests include knowledge graph, large language model, and spatio-temporal data mining. He has published more than 20 papers in international conferences and journals such as TKDE, AAAI, ICDE, ICLR, EMNLP, and KBS. He has served as a reviewer for international journals such as TKDE, AAAI, ACL, and TKDD.
